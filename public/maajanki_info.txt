4. COMPANY POLICIES & FAQS

Q: Do you offer post-launch support?
A: Yes! Every website development project includes 30 days of complimentary post-launch support covering bugs, performance tweaks, and minor text updates. Extended maintenance contracts are available starting at ₹3,000/month ($40/mo).

Q: What is your payment schedule?
A: For website and application development, we follow a milestone-based structure: 50% advance deposit to start, 30% upon approval of designs, and 20% right before final deployment/handover.

Q: How long does a standard WordPress website take?
A: A typical WordPress corporate website takes 7 to 14 business days from the receipt of content and assets. Custom web applications take 3 to 6 weeks.

Q: Are there any refunds?
A: Due to the customized nature of creative design, development, and registration filings, payments for milestones started or completed are non-refundable.

Q: Do you help with domain and hosting setup?
A: Absolutely! We help you select and purchase the right domain name and set up hosting (we recommend Hostinger, AWS, or VPS platforms). The hosting and domain subscription costs are paid directly by the client.
